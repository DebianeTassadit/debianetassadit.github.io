Large Language Models Enable Few-Shot Clustering

M.Sc. Research Project · Université Paris Cité · 2026

Reproduction and critical extension of Viswanathan et al. (2024). We benchmarked eight LLM-augmented clustering strategies on Bank77, CLINC150 and Tweet using GPT-4.1-nano for under $0.50, then proposed two novel methods — LLM Semantic Normalization and Paraphrase Ensemble — that beat all baselines on 2/3 datasets. Team of four; jury defense and A0 poster.

03

Marginalized SDA for Mixed-Type Clustering

Research Internship · Centre Borelli, CNRS · 2025

A graph-regularized marginalized stacked denoising autoencoder (mSDA) for clustering mixed numerical/categorical data. Multi-view similarity fusion (RBF + cosine) guides closed-form denoising, followed by spectral clustering. Evaluated against k-SubMix on UCI and synthetic benchmarks (NMI / ARI / accuracy) with UMAP analysis. Closed-form, CPU-fast, no iterative training.
